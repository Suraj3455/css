Biryani Trivia

When did Biryani first appear in the india?

Biryani has roots in West Asian and Mughal Persian cooking, with the modern dish's form developing in the royal kitchens of the Mughal Empire starting in the 16th century, though similar pre-Mughal rice dishes like the Tamil "Oon Soru" from 2 A.D. also existed. While legends attribute its introduction to figures like Timur in 1398 or Mumtaz Mahal, historical evidence points to a gradual evolution of Indian rice dishes combined with Persian pilaf during the Mughal reign.
